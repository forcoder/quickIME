================================================================================
API接口概览
================================================================================

InferenceEngine 类:
    Constructor/Destructor: InferenceEngine(), ~InferenceEngine()
    初始化: Initialize(const std::wstring& modelPath), Shutdown()
    模型管理: LoadModel(), UnloadModel(), IsModelLoaded()
    推理方法: GenerateSuggestions(), GenerateShortReplies(),
              GenerateWorkPhrases(), GenerateDailyExpressions()

AISuggestion 结构体:
    text: 建议文本 (std::wstring)
    type: 建议类型 (AISuggestionType)
    confidence: 置信度 (float 0.0-1.0)
    modelId: 模型ID (int64_t)
    timestamp: 时间戳

AISuggestionType 枚举:
    ShortReply, WorkPhrase, DailyExpression, ProfessionalText
